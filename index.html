Book me

Feel free to schedule an online appointment with me using this booking tool. The appointment will be set up on Microsoft Teams, and I’ll receive automatic notifications, including any cancellations. If that doesn’t work, or if you have something urgent, please write an email.

About me

I am holding a Diploma in Economics from the University of Regensburg and a Doctoral Degree (summa cum laude) from the University of Trier. I completed postgraduate studies at the Interdisciplinary Graduate Center of Excellence at the IAAEU in Trier. Prior to my current position, I worked amongst others as a research assistant to Prof. Dr. Dr. h.c. Joachim Möller at the University of Regensburg, a post-doc at the Leibniz Institute for East and Southeast European Studies (IOS) in Regensburg, and a freelancer at Charles University in Prague.

I gave lectures at various institutions, including TU Munich, University of Regensburg, Saarland University, and Universities of Applied Sciences in Frankfurt and Augsburg. Additionally, I was active abroad for the University of Cordoba in Spain, University of Perugia, and Petra Christian University in Surabaya, Indonesia. My published work can be found in international journals such as the Canadian Journal of Economics and the Stata Journal.
