CV

I am a married father of four (Rosa2014, Ignaz2016, Zita2017, Alois2020), a Professor of International Economics and Data Science at the Hochschule Fresenius in Cologne, and a passionate tennis and soccer coach. I am known for having contributed to the Stata community with the packages OSRMTIME and SXPOSE2. One was described in one of the best I speak German and English fluently. My homepage is hosted on https://hubchev.github.io.
